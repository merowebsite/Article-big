Tuesday, February 14

Who am I?

Lost and alone, in a world so wide,
A boy searches for a place to reside,
But the path ahead is shrouded in mist,
And he's left with questions he can't resist.

Who am I, and what's my purpose in life?
Why do I feel this anger and strife?
There's a fire in my heart, burning hot,
But I don't know where it's supposed to be brought.

Society tells me what I should be,
But I don't want to follow so blindly,
My passion and desire are in a fight,
And I'm struggling to see the light.

I'm angry at the world for telling me how to live,
And the pressure to conform is just too much to give,
I want to break free from the chains that bind,
And discover my true identity, so I can finally unwind.

The world's expectations are crushing my soul,
And I'm feeling lost and out of control,
But I'll keep searching until I find my way,
And let my heart guide me each and every day.

I may not know who I am, or what I want to be,
But I'll keep fighting, until I'm finally free,
From the anger and confusion that weighs me down,
And find a place where I can truly be proud.

By-5190 Riyan
